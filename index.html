    About

    I am currently a sixth-year CS Ph.D. candidate at Uninversity of Southern California, advised by Prof. Xiang Ren.

    I research on robust and efficient pre/post-training of LLMs over expanding application domains or incrementally collected data.

    Some papers I selected for myself:

    • Predicting example forgetting for continual learning of LLMs: [Arxiv'24] [ICML'24 Spotlight]
    • Model merging for LMs: [ICLR'23]
    • Continual LM pre-training: [NAACL'22]
